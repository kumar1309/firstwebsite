INDIAN CRICKET TEAM

The India men's national cricket team represent India in men's international cricket. It is governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India, and is a Full Member of the International Cricket Council with Test, One Day International and Twenty20 International status

Captains: Rohit Sharma, KL Rahul, Suryakumar Yadav, Hardik Pandya, Jasprit Bumrah, Ruturaj Gaikwad

Coaches: Rahul Dravid (Head coach),

Arenas/Stadiums: Wankhede Stadium, Eden Gardens,

Association: Board of Control for Cricket in India

First ODI: v England at Headingley, Leeds; 13 July 1974

First T20I: v South Africa at Wanderers Stadium, Johannesburg;
